Sales Pipeline Analysis

SQL Tableau RevOps Business Analysis

Analysed a SaaS sales pipeline to understand what drives uneven conversion and how revenue predictability can be improved. The project tested whether performance differences were driven by product, sector, manager, region, rep-level execution, or opportunity mix.

  • Calculated closed win rates, average deal value, time to close, and segment-level performance across products, sectors, and sales hierarchy.
  • Found that variation was more pronounced at the individual sales-agent level than across product, sector, manager, or region.
  • Built an expected revenue metric combining conversion and deal size, showing that win rate alone is an incomplete measure of commercial performance.
