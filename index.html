Sales Pipeline Analysis

SQL Business Analysis RevOps Commercial Insights

SQL analysis of a SaaS-style sales pipeline looking at what actually drives different outcomes across reps, products, sectors, and segments.

  • Calculated win rates, average deal value, time to close, and segment-level performance across products, sectors, and the sales hierarchy.
  • Found that differences were more pronounced at the individual rep level than across product, sector, manager, or region.
  • Used expected revenue logic to show why win rate on its own is a weak way of judging commercial performance.

Sales Pipeline Dashboard Suite

Tableau Dashboard Design Forecasting Stakeholder Reporting

Tableau dashboard suite built on a synthetic CRM-style dataset designed to feel closer to a real sales organisation than a typical sample file. The focus is on the questions a sales leader would actually ask: are we on track, where is risk building up, and where should attention go next?

  • Built an executive dashboard around won revenue, quota, attainment, open pipeline, weighted pipeline coverage, forecast mix, and revenue contribution by source and product.
  • Worked from a custom CRM-style dataset with 6,000 opportunities, repeat customers, multiple years of history, sales hierarchy, forecast categories, and weighted forecast logic.
  • Structured the work into executive, operational, and performance views so the dashboard follows different decision-making needs rather than dumping everything onto one page.
